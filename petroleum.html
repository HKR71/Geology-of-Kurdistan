Reservoir Types

Primary reservoir formations in Kurdistan's petroleum systems.

Carbonate Reservoirs

Mainly Cretaceous & Jurassic formations with good porosity and permeability characteristics.

  • Qamchuqa Formation
  • Sarmord Formation
  • Barsarin Formation

Clastic Reservoirs

Tertiary sandstones with variable reservoir quality depending on depositional environment.

  • Mukdadiya Formation
  • Bai Hassan Formation
  • Lower Fars Formation

Fractured Limestone

Naturally fractured reservoirs with production primarily from fracture networks.

  • Shiranish Formation
  • Bekhme Formation
  • Aqra Formation
