Key Geological Features

Tectonic Setting

Kurdistan lies within the Zagros fold-thrust belt, formed by the collision of the Arabian and Eurasian plates.

Click to learn more

Tectonic Details

  • Active continental collision zone
  • Northeastward movement of Arabian Plate
  • Convergence rate ~2-3 cm/year
  • Major faults: Main Zagros Reverse Fault

Stratigraphy

The region contains a rich succession of Paleozoic, Mesozoic, and Cenozoic sedimentary rocks.
